About

From Engineering to Business Excellence

Arun Kumar Vishwanathan is a results-driven professional who bridges the gap between technical expertise and business strategy. With a Civil Engineering foundation and an MBA in Information Management & Business Analytics from Clark University, Arun brings a unique analytical perspective to marketing and business development.

His journey began in Chennai, India, where he excelled academically, earning the Best Student Award for excellence in academics and discipline.
